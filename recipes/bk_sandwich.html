Breakfast Sandwich

Decription

My go to breakfast sandwich

Ingredients

  • bacon
  • eggs
  • bread
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Cook bacon
  2. Scramble eggs
  3. Place eggs in pan with bacon
  4. Dip both sides of bread into eggs and leave on top
  5. Once eggs have mostly cooked flip so that bread is under eggs
  6. Place cheese on eggs
  7. Fold one slice of bread onto the other
  8. Enjoy
